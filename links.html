Favorite Resources

Cool Links

HVA member-recommended links to the best astronomy resources, local clubs, publications, and dark-sky organizations.

General Astronomy

  • Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD) A different stunning image of our universe every single day, with expert commentary.
  • Heaven's Above Track the ISS, Starlink trains, satellites, and get pass predictions for your location.
  • Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL) Everything from planetary missions to near-Earth object tracking at NASA's JPL.
  • International Dark-Sky Association Preserving and protecting the nighttime environment and our heritage of dark skies.
  • American Meteor Society Upcoming meteor showers, fireball reports, and comet news.
  • Pine Mountain Observatory University of Oregon's observatory south of Bend — public nights available in summer.
  • Clear Dark Sky — Corvallis Astronomical weather forecast for Corvallis: seeing, transparency, clouds, and more.
  • Corvallis Local Weather Check before any star party — Weather Underground forecast for Corvallis, OR.

Publications

  • Astronomy Magazine Sky guides, equipment reviews, astrophotography tips, and current events in astronomy.
  • Sky & Telescope The essential resource for amateur astronomers — charts, guides, and news since 1941.

Astronomy Clubs — Pacific Northwest

  • Rose City Astronomers Portland, Oregon — one of the largest amateur astronomy clubs in the Pacific Northwest.
  • Eugene Astronomical Society Our neighbors to the south in Eugene, Oregon.
  • Central Oregon Astronomical Society Based in Bend, OR — close to some of the state's best dark skies.
  • Night Sky 45 Salem, Oregon astronomy club.
  • Seattle Astronomical Society Seattle, Washington — a well-established club with active observing programs.

Regional Star Parties

  • Oregon Star Party Annual gathering in the high desert of Eastern Oregon under some of the darkest skies in the state. Held in August.
  • Table Mountain Star Party Eastern Washington — excellent seeing at altitude, typically held in July.
  • Mt. Bachelor Star Party High-altitude dark-sky event near Bend, Oregon. Typically held in August.

Member Websites

  • Tom Carrico — CCD Images Beautiful CCD astrophotography of deep-sky objects by HVA member Tom Carrico.
