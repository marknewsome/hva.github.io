Monthly Meetings

Club meetings are held on the second Tuesday of each month at 7:00 PM PDT — either in person at Fire Station 5 or via Zoom. Check your email for the format each month.

Meetings typically include: member presentations, celestial highlights for the month, telescope tune-ups, Q&A, and club business.

All are welcome — free and open to the public. Bring questions, binoculars, or just curiosity.

Star Parties

We regularly hold in-person observing nights at Adair County Park, which offers excellent dark skies within a short drive of Corvallis.

We also host virtual star parties on Zoom — great for cloudy nights or when you can't make it out. Members share live telescope feeds, discuss targets, and enjoy the community atmosphere.
